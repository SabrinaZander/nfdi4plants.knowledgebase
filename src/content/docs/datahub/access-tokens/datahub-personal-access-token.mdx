This guide shows you how to generate a **Personal Access Token (PAT)**. A PAT can grant access to **all ARCs** of one user.

<Steps>

1. Sign in to the [DataHUB](https://git.nfdi4plants.org/)
2. Navigate to your [user settings](/nfdi4plants.knowledgebase/datahub/navigation-settings/datahub-user-settings)
2. Go to the [Access Tokens](https://git.nfdi4plants.org/-/user_settings/personal_access_tokens) settings
3. Enter or adapt the required information (more info [here](/nfdi4plants.knowledgebase/datahub/access-tokens#token-information))
4. Click <btn>Create personal access token</btn> (4)

    ![](@images/datahub/datahub-access-token01.drawio.png)

5. `Your new personal access token` appears on top. Copy (1) and use it in the application 

    ![](@images/datahub/datahub-access-token02.drawio.png)

</Steps>
