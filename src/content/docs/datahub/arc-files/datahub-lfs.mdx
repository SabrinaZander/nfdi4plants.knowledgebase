## LFS Flag

Open your ARC in the DataHUB and navigate to the folder with LFS objects. Files uploaded with LFS are flagged as "LFS" (1).

![](@images/datahub/datahub-lfs-flagged.drawio.png)

## Project Storage

You can easily check the usage quota used by your ARC by navigating to your ARC in the DataHUB and clicking on "Project Storage" in the right sidebar (1).

![](@images/datahub/datahub-lfs-project-storage.drawio.png)
